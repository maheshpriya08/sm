About Sanmahi Solutions

Reliable work,
from start to finish.

Sanmahi Solutions brings painting, PVC interiors and roofing together under one accountable team, working across Kumbakonam and Chennai.

Our story

Built on trades that
work well together.

Sanmahi Solutions started from a simple observation: most home and building projects touch painting, interior fit-out and roofing at some point, yet homeowners usually end up hiring separate contractors for each — coordinating schedules, chasing quality across teams, and absorbing the gaps between them.

We built Sanmahi Solutions to close those gaps. By keeping painting, PVC works and roofing under one team, we can plan a project properly from day one, sequence the trades so nothing gets damaged or redone, and stand behind the finished result as a whole — not just our piece of it.

Today we work with homeowners, families and small businesses across Kumbakonam and Chennai, on everything from a single feature wall to a full terrace roofing job with interior PVC fit-out to match.

How we work

A straightforward
process, every time.

01

Enquiry & site visit

Share your project details on WhatsApp, by phone or through our contact form. For most jobs we'll arrange a site visit to understand the space and your requirements first-hand.

02

Free estimate

You'll receive a clear estimate covering materials, labour and an expected timeline — no vague pricing or hidden line items added later.

03

Scheduling

Once you're ready to proceed, we confirm a start date and sequence the work so painting, PVC and roofing trades don't get in each other's way.

04

Execution

Our team carries out the work with regular updates, keeping the site tidy and flagging anything that needs your input along the way.

05

Handover & finishing check

We walk the completed project with you, address any final touch-ups, and hand over with care and maintenance guidance where relevant.
